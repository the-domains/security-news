University of Central Florida computer network hacked: 63,000 affected

The University of Central Florida on Thursday announced a massive data breach, saying hackers swiped about 63,000 Social Security numbers from students and staffers. The University of Central Florida posted a statement on its website Feb. 4 that "a recent outside intrusion into UCF's computer network compromised the personally identifiable information of some members of our university community.
